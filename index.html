ABOUT US

A causal and one of a kind social pub

Set in the heart of downtown London, Ballmer's Peak strives to cater to the hard working tech, business and creative professionals of the city. We facilitate and host many different meet-ups so like minded people can connect, enjoy a pint and grab a bite. We are a safe, all-inclusive space to socialize, relax and have fun!
